Quantum Runner

Platform: PC, Mobile Engine: Unity 2022.3 Development Time: 8 months

Quantum Runner revolutionizes the platformer genre by incorporating real quantum mechanics concepts into gameplay. Players can exist in multiple states simultaneously, tunnel through barriers, and manipulate probability to overcome seemingly impossible challenges.
