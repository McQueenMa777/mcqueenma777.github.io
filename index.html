Under Pressure

Platform: PC Engine: Unreal Engine Development Time: 2 months

Under Pressure is a 2D (made with 3D assets) Point & Click game in a marine exploration theme. In the game, you will be playing a submarine to collect different fish (and some unknown creatures as you go deeper!) to check the top-right boxes. Each fish has its own pop-up introduction when collected. Your fuel is also running out on the top-left. Make sure you survive this mission!

Key Features

  • Pixelated artstyle and nostalgic post-processing textures
  • Low-poly 3D models, pixel UIs, retro colouring
  • Accessibility functions including navigation arrow and cursor
  • Customized "Fishypedia" for each creature captured
  • A count-down system and pressure gauge to hunt the player down

The game features a unique visual style that represents a retro and nostalgic vibe, as well as providing an uncanny and unsettling feeling while encountering some normal creatures first and then weird ones. Push yourself to the limit and see how many you can get!

Echo

Platform: PC Engine: Unreal Engine Development Time: 4 months

Echo is a 2D art game with strong narratives and a sentimental, reflective, and hopeful theme. In the game, you will be playing as Giovanni, a street guitarist, who helps passers-by unwind their dilemmas by playing songs. Through conversations, you understand their emotional burdens and choose the right song. The game features a side-scrolling guitar level where you strike nodes to play music while avoiding negative emotions appearing on the strings. Your goal is to genuinely help people and earn a living through your music.

Key Features

  • Strong narrative-driven gameplay where player saves each lost soul
  • Rhythm-based guitar mechanics with varied musical challenges and escalating complexity
  • Intertwined dialogues that immerse the player into the inner world at the bottom of their hearts
  • Season cycles to signify the fleet of time and the changes reflected by each individual
  • Different and corresponding challenges based on different narratives from each character

Immerse yourself in this 2D art game where you, as a street guitarist named Giovanni, use the power of music to resolve the emotional struggles of passers-by, finding hope and connection in a world shaped by your melodies.

CATastrophy

Platform: PC Engine: Unreal Engine Development Time: 2 days

CATastrophy (a submission to Climax Game Jam 2025) is a 2D plane shooter game where you play a lovely cat astronaut solider (it has different outfits!) to shoot enemies coming from different directions (cows, boots, wheels, anything you can think of!). The movement of the cat is determined by the recoil of the gun. Try it and see what score you can get!

Kindle

Platform: PC Engine: Unreal Engine Development Time: 1 month

Kindle is a 2D nostalgic and sketchy style game that uses your audio input to draw pictures. In the game, you will play as a blind daughter’s father and envision how the world looks like for you curious daughter. You will be able to appreciate your own work at the very end!

Key Features

  • Pre-trained audio recognition model in Python
  • Technical stack to impport the Python model into UE
  • Touching narratives betweeen the father and the daughter
  • Customizable brush size and shape
  • Ability to generate your own artwork at the end

Kindle unleashes the creativity of the player in the form of a touching and deeply sentimental conversation between a father and a daughter. If you could, please show your daughter how the outside looks like, fulfilling her vulnerable dream.

ATCG

Platform: Offline Engine: N/A Development Time: 2 months

ATCG (Arcane Trading Card Game) is an in-real-life, turn-based, 1V1, and tradable card games based on the lore of the Arcane series. Card type includes Champion, Equippment, Spell, Resource and Event. The ultimate goal is to exhaust your opponent’s resource!

逝者如斯

Platform: PC Engine: Unreal Engine 5 Development Time: 1 months

逝者如斯 (Passage Like Running Water) is a 3D immersive experience in which the player experiences a life journey of a baby in cradle, a footballer child, a man stressed with work and an elderly walking out of hospital audiovisually from the perspective of a drifting paperboat.

逝者如斯 is a 3D immersive journey through the stages of life, seen from the drifting perspective of a paper boat. From a cradle to a hospital exit, light and sound quietly trace the flow of time.
